Gil Scott Heron

The Godfather of Hip Hop and Rap music

  • Gil Scott Heron, born on April 1949, is considered as one of the Fathers of modern Hip Hop and Rap music.
  • He started his career in the '60s and '70s as a Spoken Word artist, basically reciting poetry on an instrumental base.
  • His song "The Revolution will not be televised", apart from being still as popular as it was back then, it is considered as a classic in Hip Hop culture.
  • This paved the way for Hip Hop culture and Rap music to arise more and more later on.
  • "The Bottle", song released in 1973, is among his most famous tracks
  • He dies at the age of 62 in New York in 2011.
